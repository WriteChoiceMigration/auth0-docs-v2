You can use the <Tooltip tip="Auth0 Dashboard: Auth0's main product to configure your services." cta="View Glossary" href="/docs/glossary?term=Auth0+Dashboard">Auth0 Dashboard</Tooltip> to update your billing information. This feature is only available for tenant administrators on any tenant-based subscriptions. For account-based subscriptions, you must be a team owner to access this functionality.

## 請求と支払

請求情報を更新する方法：

1. Navigate to [Auth0 Dashboard > Settings](https://manage.auth0.com/#/dashboard/settings).

   <Callout icon="file-lines" color="#0EA5E9" iconType="regular">

   If you are using a Team, you can find this setting under **Teams > Settings > Subscriptions**.

   </Callout>
2. Under **Tenant Settings**,select **Payment & Billing**.
3. Click **Add Payment Details** to input your payment information.

4. Click **Update** to save your changes.
