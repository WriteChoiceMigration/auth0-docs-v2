Auth0のサブスクリプションはいつでもダウングレードやキャンセルできます。状況に応じて、次回の請求サイクルでクレジット受け取ったり、払い戻しを受けたりできることがあります。

## セルフサービスプラン

If you are subscribed to an Essential or Professional plan, you can downgrade or cancel your subscription in the [Auth0 Dashboard](https://manage.auth0.com/#).

### プランをダウングレードまたはキャンセルする

1. Go to [Dashboard > Settings](https://manage.auth0.com/#/tenant), and then switch to the **Subscription** view.
2. Locate the plan you'd like to downgrade to, and then select **Downgrade**.
3. If you want to cancel your plan, locate the **Free** plan, and then select **Downgrade**.

   <Frame>![Auth0 Tenant Settings Subscription tab](/images/cdy7uua7fh8z/3yISRVTJOwP4Yo2uG0iZdO/3ffec2e74f090fbcecc3da052fb08ce4/Screenshot_2024-12-06_at_17.10.02.png)</Frame>
4. To confirm your subscription change, select **Subscribe Now**.

### クレジットまたは払い戻しをリクエストする

If you downgrade or cancel your Auth0 subscription, you may be eligible for a credit on your next billing cycle or a refund. To learn more, read our [Billing Policy](/docs/troubleshoot/customer-support/operational-policies/billing-policy).

## エンタープライズプラン

If you are subscribed to an Enterprise plan, contact your Technical Account Manager (TAM) and then [open a ticket with Auth0 Support](/docs/troubleshoot/customer-support/open-and-manage-support-tickets).
