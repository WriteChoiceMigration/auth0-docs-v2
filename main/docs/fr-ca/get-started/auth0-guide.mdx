Guide is an AI-powered chatbot that answers your questions about Auth0. It sources information from our [Documentation](https://auth0.com/docs), [Blog](https://auth0.com/blog), and [Community](http://community.auth0.com). For Enterprise subscription customers, Guide also leverages Auth0 tenant data from [Security Center](/docs/secure/security-center) to help you monitor, troubleshoot, and implement tenant security best practices.

Guide answers your questions, such as "How do I set up passkeys?" with a summarized answer and links to relevant documentation and [incident playbooks](/docs/secure/attack-protection/playbooks) for tenant and user security.

## Comment fonctionne Guide

Guide analyse votre demande pour identifier ce que vous recherchez, consulte les sources d’information pertinentes d’Auth0, puis génère une réponse résumée basée sur ses trouvailles, accompagnée de liens vers les documents associés.

You can ask follow-up questions, [validate its responses](#how-does-guide-avoid-ai-hallucinations%3F) using the provided links, and give feedback on Guide by rating its responses.

For Enterprise subscription customers: If you [configure security monitoring alerts](/docs/secure/security-center/security-alerts) in Security Center, Guide helps you troubleshoot incidents and suggests security guidance from a relevant [incident playbook](/docs/secure/attack-protection/playbooks).

For example, as shown in the following screenshot, when a significant bot surge triggers your <Tooltip tip="Bot Detection: Form of attack protection in which Auth0 blocks suspected bot traffic by enabling a CAPTCHA during the login process." cta="View Glossary" href="/docs/glossary?term=Bot+Detection">Bot Detection</Tooltip> alert, Guide recommends remediation steps from the [Bot Detection Attack playbook](/docs/secure/attack-protection/playbooks/bot-detection-playbook), such as activating CAPTCHA for affected authentication flows, and prompts you to inspect relevant events in the tenant logs.

## Guide peut répondre aux questions relatives au produit et à la plate-forme Auth0, telles que :

You can access Guide by selecting the **Ask Guide** button in the top-right corner of the <Tooltip tip="Auth0 Dashboard: Auth0's main product to configure your services." cta="View Glossary" href="/docs/glossary?term=Auth0+Dashboard">Auth0 Dashboard</Tooltip>.

When you select **Ask Guide**, Guide pops up, prompting you to ask about Auth0 features. It may suggest questions relevant to your current location in the Auth0 Dashboard. For example, if you’re under **Branding > <Tooltip tip="Universal Login: Your application redirects to Universal Login, hosted on Auth0's Authorization Server, to verify a user's identity." cta="View Glossary" href="/docs/glossary?term=Universal+Login">Universal Login</Tooltip>**, Guide will prompt you with questions about Universal Login, such as:

* Combien d’utilisateurs se sont connectés à mon application le mois dernier?
* Questions auxquelles Guide ne peut répondre

## Comment Guide évite-t-il les hallucinations de l’IA?

You can disable Guide in your tenant by contacting [Auth0 Support](https://support.auth0.com/).

## Data security in Guide

Your messages are safeguarded by Okta’s enterprise-grade security and compliance standards.

Guide does:

* Use provided messages to search public Auth0 sources for relevant information.

Guide does not:

* Access your account or tenant data.
* Use provided messages for LLM training purposes.
* Share your messages with third parties.

## How does Guide avoid AI hallucinations?

Guide builds responses using content authored by Auth0 and includes links to the documents referenced in all responses. You can follow any referenced link to verify the original source data and learn additional information.
